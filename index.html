Appetizers

  • Buffalo Cauliflower Wings : veggie riff on buffalo chicken wings is super easy to make in the oven!
  • Deviled Eggs : Garnish them with chopped chives, dill, and/or smoked paprika for a fun and festive look.
  • Stuffed Mushrooms: These bite-sized appetizers are simple and flavorful, filled with panko bread crumbs, pine nuts, parsley, sun-dried tomatoes, and cheese.

Main Course

  • Ground Beef Stir Fry:This tender ground beef stir fry is an easy meal full of fresh veggies, with a flavor-packed sauce that coats every bite. We enjoy this with jasmine rice, but if noodles are your thing, go for it.
  • Hibachi Chicken and Rice Casserole:This hibachi something is a one-skillet dinner that goes from stovetop to table. Boneless chicken thighs, zucchini, and broccoli baked with instant rice make a family-friendly dinner with minimal effort.
  • Pork and Apple Meatballs in Bao-Style Buns:Purchased prepared pizza dough offers a shortcut to making tender bao-style buns at home.
